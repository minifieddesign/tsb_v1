Pail Design

Branding and marketing strategy

My time at Pail was an exciting period. Never before had I been so hands-on with so many aspects of a project or working with clients. I came in during the early stages of the TCMS corporate rebrand, helping to flesh out their print collateral and vehicle wraps. With Sonoma Cider, we worked off branding established by Sandstrom Partners to develop an event booth setup, sell sheets, keg labels, and other marketing materials. With BrandLive, the initial branding had been similarly established by Fiction design agency, giving us a starting point for how to navigate their marketing collateral.
