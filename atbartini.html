Bartini

A Martini Bar

These martini glasses are more like goblets, full of vibrant colors and a virtual buffet of ingredients and add-ins. A bar known for its dozens of large, colorful cocktails and never-ending happy hour, Bartini appeals to a younger audience, so the website needed to be designed with mobile in mind. The photographs feature saturated hues against the bar's signature black speckled tabletops, inspiring the icon set typifying what Bartini is truly known for: its countless martinis.

Food & Drink Menus

Art Direction, Design, Photography

With five pages filled with drink options, these menu books were always on the table. Their thick hard covers kept the pages relatively clean, the front page advertising Bartini's signature specials. Printed front and back, the waterproof black food menus could be easily wiped off and shared between guests.
