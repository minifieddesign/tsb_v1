A Restaurant and Whiskey Bar

On either sides of a hotel lobby in downtown—a restaurant on one side and a whiskey bar on the other—Swank n’ Swine is up against some of the best dining options in the city. The entire brand needed a refresh to reflect the delicious platings coming out of the kitchen.

Menus for Swine Moonshine and Whiskey Bar

Boasting one of the largest whiskey selections in downtown, Swine Bar menus were inevitably a multi-page affair. These custom wooden boards kept the spirit list, drinks, food, and happy hour menus all in one place, while the leather covers keep the pages clean even in during the busiest times.
